There are actually three variations:

  1. https://vzome.com/app/?editMode=plane is the working plane proof-of-concept. You cannot change the plane, save, undo, etc…. just build.
  2. https://vzome.com/app/?editMode=other is actually able to execute some vZome edit code, but offers no way to manually build struts. There’s a hidden menu of commands, most of which will fail.
  3. https://vzome.com/app/ is the original, and it can open almost all vZome files, but it is REALLY SLOW.
